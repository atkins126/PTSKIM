Nodes 3..6 are the nodes corresponding to nodes 1..4 in the original Spiess-Florian network.
As the node numbers in the Spiess-Florian.lin input file have not been changed compared to the original network, the Offset is set to -2 in the control file.

Zone 1 is connected to node 3 (node 1 in the original network) by a 12 minutes walk link.
Zone 2 is connected to node 6 (node 4 in the original network) by a 12 minutes walk link.
